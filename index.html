About Me

I'm a Backend Software Engineer at Wells Fargo — one of the largest banks in the world — working on backend applications for trade finance, supply chain finance, international SWIFT wire payments, and sanctions monitoring. Systems at this scale leave very little room for error, and that environment has sharpened how I think about reliability and correctness.

I graduated from NIT Allahabad — one of India's premier engineering institutes — in 2024 with a B.Tech in Information Technology. Outside of my day job I'm always building: production-deployed CRMs, agentic AI pipelines, hackathon projects. I'm a LeetCode Knight with 1,000+ problems solved and genuinely love hard technical problems.

Outside of work, I read a lot — it's probably my favourite thing. I love cricket, music, dance, and I occasionally upload my vocals on Instagram.
